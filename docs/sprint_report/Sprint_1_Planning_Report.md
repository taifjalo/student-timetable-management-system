## Sprint Goal

The goal of Sprint 1 is to establish the project foundation.
This includes forming the team, defining the project topic, creating the product vision and product goal, setting the project objectives, establishing the product backlog, planning the work, and setting up Trello for project management.

- Creation and initial prioritization of the Product Backlog (user stories)

## Team Capacity & Assumptions

For Sprint 1, the team members agreed to:

- Work a few hours on **Sundays and Mondays** during the two-week Sprint 1 period
- Meetings are conducted via **Discord**
- Smaller meetings may occur when at least two members are available
- Working hours are recorded individually in a **shared Excel file**
- All development and planning activities are performed collaboratively during meetings and individual work sessions.

**Additional note:**
Initial planning artifacts, including the **Sprint 1 Planning and Review docs**, **Product Goal & Backlog & Sprint 1 boards**, **Trello Workspace setup**, and a **draft Use Case Diagram**, were prepared by the **Scrum Master** to support efficient collaboration. These artifacts were later reviewed, refined, and agreed upon by the entire team.

## Definition of Done

A Sprint 1 task is considered done when:

- The task objectives are completed and reviewed by the team.
- The related artifact (document, diagram, or setup) is finalized.
- The task is moved to the "Done" column in Trello.
- Relevant documentation is available in the project repository.

_Definition of done is specified in every Trello stories._
