## Demo Summary (What Was Shown in the Review)

During the Sprint 1 review, the team presented the main planning and preparation outcomes of the sprint. The demo covered the Trello workspace structure, including the Product Backlog and Sprint 1 setup, as well as the defined Product Vision, goals, and objectives.

The team also demonstrated the initial user stories with story point estimations, the Sprint 1 Planning Report, and the UML / Use Case Diagram, which was refined through team discussion into a final version. The review confirmed that the project foundation is complete and the team is ready to move forward to implementation in the next sprint.
